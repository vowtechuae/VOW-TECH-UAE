// DATA RISKS YOU FACE

Common Data Loss Threats for UAE Businesses

Without a robust cloud backup and DR strategy, a single unexpected event can permanently destroy years of business data and bring operations to a complete halt.

01
Ransomware & Cyberattacks

Ransomware incidents have surged across the UAE — encrypting entire server environments and demanding payment for data release. Without immutable, offsite backups, businesses face an impossible choice between paying or losing everything.

02
Hardware Failure & Corruption

Server hard drives, RAID arrays, and storage controllers fail without warning. When a drive dies and your only backup lives on the same physical hardware, data recovery becomes costly, incomplete, and sometimes impossible.

03
Human Error & Accidental Deletion

The most common cause of data loss isn't malicious — it's accidental. Employees deleting the wrong file, overwriting critical data, or misconfiguring a system can cause irreversible damage without versioned, frequent backups in place.

04
Physical Disaster & Site Outage

Fire, flooding, power surges, and prolonged utility failures can destroy on-premises infrastructure entirely. A UAE business relying solely on local backups risks losing everything if its primary site becomes inaccessible or uninhabitable.

05
Extended Downtime & Revenue Loss

Without a tested DR plan, recovering from any significant incident takes days or weeks — during which your business cannot operate. Industry research consistently shows that extended downtime costs UAE SMEs more than the incident itself.

06
Compliance & Regulatory Exposure

UAE data protection regulations, DIFC, ADGM, and sector-specific requirements mandate that businesses maintain secure, recoverable data backups. A breach or data loss event without demonstrable backup compliance carries significant legal and financial risk.

// HOW WE WORK

Our Backup & DR Implementation Process

A structured five-step process from data audit to live production backup — delivering reliable protection with zero disruption to your operations.

01
Data Assessment & Audit

We map every data source across your environment — servers, databases, endpoints, SaaS applications — documenting volumes, criticality rankings, current backup status, and retention requirements to form the foundation of your protection strategy.

02
Backup Policy & DR Design

We define RTO and RPO targets for each workload tier, design a backup schedule and retention policy, select the appropriate platform and storage architecture, and document a complete DR runbook — presented as a clear, transparent proposal before any work begins.

03
Cloud Configuration & Setup

Our engineers deploy and configure backup agents, cloud storage repositories, replication jobs, and alerting rules — using pre-tested templates to accelerate deployment and minimise configuration errors. All credentials and encryption keys are documented and securely stored.

04
Testing & Verification

Every backup job is verified via test restores in an isolated sandbox environment — confirming data integrity, measuring actual restore times against RTO targets, and validating that every critical system can be brought back cleanly before we sign off the project.

05
Ongoing Monitoring & Support

Post-deployment, we actively monitor backup job completion, storage utilisation, and replication health 24/7 — resolving issues proactively and delivering monthly health reports so you always have full visibility of your data protection posture.
