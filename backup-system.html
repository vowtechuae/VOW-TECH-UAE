// THE RISKS

Why Businesses Need Proper Backup Systems

Without a robust backup system, a single incident can bring your entire operation to a standstill.

01
Ransomware & Cyberattacks

Ransomware can encrypt all your data in minutes. Without an isolated, immutable backup system, paying the ransom becomes the only option — often with no guarantee of recovery.

02
Hardware Failure

Hard drives and servers fail without warning. A single RAID failure or server crash can destroy years of business data — unless a properly tested backup system is in place.

03
Human Error & Accidental Deletion

Accidental file deletion or overwrites account for a significant portion of data loss incidents. Granular backup systems allow you to restore any file, folder, or database to a precise point in time.

Our 5-Step Backup System Deployment

A proven, structured process that ensures your backup system is running and verified from day one.

01
Infrastructure Audit

We inventory all servers, endpoints, databases, and applications to define scope, data volumes, and criticality tiers.

02
System Architecture

We design the optimal backup architecture — storage sizing, retention schedules, replication topology, and failover strategy aligned to your RPO and RTO.

03
Hardware & Software Deploy

Installation and configuration of backup servers, NAS/SAN, cloud connectors, and backup agents across all protected systems — zero disruption to live operations.

04
Recovery Validation

We perform full restoration tests — file-level, application-level, and bare-metal — and document results against your agreed RTO targets before sign-off.

05
Ongoing Management

24/7 monitoring, monthly health checks, quarterly recovery tests, and detailed reporting to keep your backup system current and fully operational.
