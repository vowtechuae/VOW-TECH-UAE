// THE PROBLEM

Common Backup &
Data Challenges

Unprotected data is a ticking time bomb. Here's what businesses face without a proper backup strategy.

Ransomware Attacks

Ransomware can encrypt your entire data set in minutes. Without air-gapped backups, recovery is impossible.

Hardware Failure

Hard drives and servers fail without warning. No backup means permanent data loss and extended downtime.

Human Error

Accidental deletions and overwrites are the leading cause of data loss. Versioned backups allow instant rollback.

Natural Disasters

Fire, flooding, or power surges can destroy on-site servers. Offsite and cloud replication keeps you covered.

Compliance Gaps

UAE regulations require data retention policies. Lack of proper backup puts you at legal and regulatory risk.

// FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is the difference between backup and disaster recovery?
Backup is the process of copying data so it can be restored after loss. Disaster recovery (DR) is a broader plan that covers the full restoration of IT systems and business operations after a major event — including failover, failback, and continuity procedures. VOWTECH provides both as part of an integrated strategy.
How quickly can you restore our data after a failure?
With Veeam Instant VM Recovery, virtual machines can be restored and running within minutes directly from the backup file. Physical servers and full environments have defined RTOs based on your contract — typically ranging from 15 minutes to 4 hours depending on your chosen tier.
Are our backups protected against ransomware?
Yes. VOWTECH implements immutable backup storage and air-gapped offsite copies that cannot be modified or deleted by ransomware. Even if your primary systems are fully compromised, your backup data remains intact and recoverable.
Do you back up Microsoft 365 data like emails and Teams?
Absolutely. Microsoft's shared responsibility model means they do not guarantee data recovery for deletions or corruption. VOWTECH uses Veeam Backup for Microsoft 365 to protect Exchange Online, SharePoint, OneDrive, and Teams — with granular item-level restore.
How often are backups tested for recoverability?
For Professional and Enterprise clients, VOWTECH conducts monthly restore verification tests — automatically booting backup images in an isolated environment to confirm they are fully recoverable. You receive a monthly report confirming backup integrity.
Can you support our existing backup infrastructure?
Yes. Our engineers support and extend existing backup environments including Veeam, Acronis, Commvault, Veritas, and others. We can manage, optimise, and add cloud replication to your current setup without requiring a full replacement.
