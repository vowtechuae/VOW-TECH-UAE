// The Threat Landscape

Cybersecurity Challenges Facing UAE Businesses

Cyberattacks in the Middle East are rising fast. Without the right protection, one breach can cost millions — and destroy trust overnight.

Rising Ransomware & Malware

UAE organisations are increasingly targeted by sophisticated ransomware groups. Without next-gen endpoint protection and offline backups, recovery can cost millions in ransom or downtime.

Insider Threats & Credential Theft

Compromised accounts and malicious insiders represent one of the most damaging and hardest-to-detect attack vectors — requiring identity-aware access controls and behavioural monitoring.

Unprotected Cloud & Remote Environments

The rapid adoption of cloud and remote work has expanded the attack surface. Misconfigured cloud services and unpatched endpoints are the most common entry points for attackers today.
