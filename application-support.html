Our Application Support Services

Six layers of application management — keeping your software optimised, available, and aligned to your business needs around the clock.

01

Application Maintenance

Scheduled and ad-hoc maintenance keeps your applications current, stable, and secure — covering patches, updates, configuration management, and bug resolution.

  • Patch & version management
  • Bug identification & resolution
  • Configuration drift correction
  • Scheduled maintenance windows
02

Performance Monitoring

Real-time monitoring across application layers detects slowdowns, resource contention, and anomalies before they impact users — with automated alerts and instant escalation.

  • 24/7 synthetic & real-user monitoring
  • Response time & throughput tracking
  • Resource utilisation dashboards
  • Automated threshold alerting
03

Helpdesk & Troubleshooting

Multi-tier helpdesk support gives users a single point of contact for all application issues — with guaranteed SLA response times and escalation to senior engineers when needed.

  • L1 / L2 / L3 tiered support
  • Ticketing & issue tracking
  • SLA-guaranteed response times
  • Remote & on-site resolution
04

Performance Optimisation

Deep-dive analysis of application performance bottlenecks — database query tuning, caching strategies, and resource rightsizing to deliver faster, more responsive applications.

  • Database query optimisation
  • Memory & CPU profiling
  • Caching & load distribution
  • Capacity planning & scaling
05

Integration & API Support

Expert management of application integrations, APIs, and middleware — keeping your connected ecosystem in sync and resolving data pipeline failures before they cascade.

  • API health monitoring
  • Middleware & ESB management
  • Data pipeline integrity checks
  • Third-party connector support
06

Reporting & Continuous Improvement

Monthly service reports, SLA scorecards, and trend analysis provide full visibility into application health — informing ongoing improvements and strategic roadmap decisions.

  • Monthly SLA & health reports
  • Incident trend analysis
  • Change & release documentation
  • Roadmap & improvement planning
