// THE RISKS

Why Traditional Systems Fail Businesses

Manual registers, PINs, and swipe cards leave your workforce management exposed to fraud, inaccuracy, and compliance risk.

01
Buddy Punching & Time Fraud

PIN and card-based attendance systems are easily abused — colleagues clock in for absent employees. Studies suggest buddy punching costs businesses up to 2.2% of their annual payroll. Biometrics make this physically impossible.

02
Lost Cards & Shared PINs

Access cards get lost, stolen, or lent to contractors. Shared PINs circulate widely in seconds. Without biometric verification, you have no way to confirm the person at the door is actually authorised to be there.

03
Inaccurate HR & Payroll Data

Paper registers and manual time-sheets are riddled with errors, disputes, and deliberate manipulation. Without verified attendance data, HR teams waste hours reconciling records — and payroll mistakes generate legal exposure.
