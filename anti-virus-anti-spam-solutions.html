Common Phishing Threats

Modern phishing attacks are sophisticated, targeted, and increasingly hard for end users to identify without the right protection in place.

Business Email Compromise (BEC)

Attackers impersonate executives or vendors to trick employees into transferring funds or sharing sensitive data — costing UAE businesses millions annually.

Credential Harvesting

Fake login pages mimic Microsoft 365, banking portals, and corporate systems — silently stealing usernames and passwords to enable deeper network compromise.

Malicious Links & Attachments

Zero-day malware, ransomware payloads, and weaponised Office documents delivered via email — a single click can encrypt your entire network within minutes.

Our Anti-Phishing Services

Six layers of email and web protection — blocking threats before, during, and after delivery to your organisation.

01

Advanced Email Filtering

Multi-layer inbound and outbound email filtering blocks spam, phishing, and malware before messages reach end users' inboxes.

  • SPF, DKIM & DMARC enforcement
  • Reputation-based sender scoring
  • Header & content analysis
  • Bulk & graymail filtering
02

AI-Based Threat Detection

Machine learning models analyse email patterns, sender behaviour, and payload characteristics to detect novel phishing campaigns in real time.

  • Behavioural anomaly detection
  • Lookalike domain identification
  • Zero-day phishing detection
  • Continuous model retraining
03

URL & Link Protection

Every link in every email is scanned and sandboxed at click time — blocking malicious redirects, drive-by downloads, and credential-harvesting pages.

  • Real-time URL rewriting & scanning
  • Safe Links (Microsoft 365 native)
  • Malicious redirect detection
  • Click-time page rendering
04

Attachment Sandboxing

Suspicious attachments are detonated in an isolated cloud sandbox before delivery — analysing behaviour and blocking ransomware, macros, and zero-day exploits.

  • Dynamic detonation analysis
  • Office macro & script detection
  • PDF & archive scanning
  • Safe Attachments (M365 native)
05

User Awareness & Simulation

Simulated phishing campaigns and awareness training transform your staff from the weakest link into your strongest line of defence.

  • Phishing simulation campaigns
  • Targeted security awareness training
  • Click-rate & risk reporting
  • Customised training modules
06

Monitoring, Alerts & Response

24/7 SOC-integrated monitoring with instant threat alerts, incident response, and detailed reporting dashboards for your security team.

  • Real-time threat alerts
  • Incident investigation & response
  • Monthly security posture reports
  • SIEM & SOC integration
